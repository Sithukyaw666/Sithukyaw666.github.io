# Hello, World (I Have No Idea What I'm Doing)

So I added a blog. Nobody asked for this. I barely asked for this. And yet here we are.

The plan is to write about things I do, things I break, things I _think_ I understand but definitely don't, and occasionally something that accidentally works and I have no idea why.

## Why a Blog?

Honestly? I kept doing random stuff and immediately forgetting all of it. Thought writing it down would help. Spoiler: it probably won't, but at least there'll be a paper trail when something inevitably catches fire.

## What to Expect

- Lab stuff that made sense at 2am and make zero sense at 9am
- Docker setups that work on my machine and absolutely nowhere else
- Backend code written with confidence by someone who ask Claude 80% of it
- Kubernetes configs that were copy-pasted from Stack Overflow and have been running in production for 8 months untouched because I'm too scared to update them
- Networking stuff I barely understand

## Why Is This Built the Way It Is

I used JavaScript, fetched some markdown files, and threw `marked.js` at them. I could not explain to you how it works at a fundamental level. It renders. I left it alone. That's engineering.

Could I have used Astro? Sure. Would I have understood what Astro was doing? Absolutely not. So we're doing it this way.
